How this works...

French verbs are opinionated about prepositions. Some demand à, some demand de, some want nothing at all — and a handful change meaning entirely depending on which you use.

Each sentence shows the verb in real context. After you reveal, you'll see the breakdown and a note explaining the logic. Say everything out loud — your mouth needs to learn this, not just your brain.

Pick a category to focus, or choose Mix to see verbs from all categories in random order — the verb and its preposition stay hidden until you reveal.
