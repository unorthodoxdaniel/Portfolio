How this works...

Think of French numbers as building blocks, not memorisation. You already know the blocks — we're just going to stack them differently.

The key insight: French counts in chunks. Sixty-nineteen (79), four-twenties (80), four-twenty-ten (90). Once the logic clicks, the numbers build themselves.
