How to use this...

Each drill below targets a specific area of French that becomes instinctive through repeated, contextualised exposure — not memorisation. That's the Noble method: you encounter real sentences, work out the answer, then see the logic explained.

Start wherever you feel weakest. Numbers if you still hesitate counting. Pronouns if word order trips you up. Articles if du/de la/au/aux blur together. Every drill has audio, a recycling queue for missed items, and a score tracker.

All drills work offline once loaded. Open one, bookmark it, and come back daily — ten minutes a day beats two hours once a week.
