Les Pronoms

Four-Part Pronoun Drill — Noble Method

How this works...

Four drills, each targeting a different pronoun type: stressed, reflexive, relative, and demonstrative. Run them in any order — or tackle all four in one sitting.

Each sentence gives you the English. Say the French out loud before revealing the answer. Hearing yourself speak it is the point — that's how the Noble method builds fluency.

Mark Got it when you got there correctly, or Recycle to see it again at the end of the set.

Audio uses your browser's speech synthesis (French voice).
Choose any drill from the menu — your progress is tracked per set.
