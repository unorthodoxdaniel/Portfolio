à, au, de, du, des & beyond — Noble Method

How this works...

French articles are not random — but the same letters do different jobs depending on context. Du can mean of the (contracted de + le) or some (partitive). Des can mean of the / from the or some. The trick is learning to recognise which job each one is doing.

Every card tells you which rule is at play after you reveal — so you're always building understanding, not just memorising. Say each sentence out loud.

Start with Tier 1 to build the foundation, then work up. Use Mix once you feel ready to be tested without labels.
