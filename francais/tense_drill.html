How this works...

Imparfait

The "used to / was doing" tense. Irregular verbs just swap in a new stem — the endings never change. Learn the stem, the rest is free.

Passé Composé

The "I did / I have done" past. avoir or être + past participle. Regular participles are easy; a handful of irregulars are worth memorising cold.

Futur Proche

The "going to" future. Just aller + infinitive. You already know aller — this tense costs almost nothing new.

Futur Simple

The "will" future. Irregular verbs swap their stem but keep the same endings. Learn the stem once, use it forever.
