Paragraph structure — 4 slots

Slot 1
Claim
Position + conditional verb + optional scope qualifier
Always conditional — never present tense
Slot 2
En effet
Mechanism: what happens to people in general (on)
Conditional preferred. Present = grounded but weaker
Slot 3
Par exemple
Concrete person (qui) — grounds the claim
Always present tense — no conditional here
Slot 4
C'est pourquoi
Solution or conclusion — returns to conditional
il serait essentiel > il est essentiel
Register rule: Present tense = asserting a fact (B1). Conditional = arguing a position (B2/C1). Anywhere you have a present tense verb in slots 1, 2 or 4 — ask if it should be conditional.

Modal swap rule: Both pourrait = strongest register. One swap (keep present for on se retrouve) = grounded. Default: both conditional. Use one swap when slot 3 is very concrete.

Slot 4 — Conclusion

C'est pourquoi il serait essentiel de [solution].
Three shapes

C'est la raison pour laquelle beaucoup de [personnes] cherchent à…Behavioural observation.
C'est pourquoi il est important de ne pas oublier que…Nuance or caveat. Use for balanced conclusion.

Connectors & fixed phrases

Setup phrases — slot 2 openers
à force de + infinitive
by dint of / by constantly doing
Sets up drift. à force de + verb → on finit par… e.g. À force de tout gérer seul, on finit par s'épuiser.
lorsqu'on + verb
when one / whenever someone
Standard slot 2 opener. Introduces the triggering situation neutrally.
en effet
indeed / in fact
Signals slot 2 opening. Introduces the explanation of the slot 1 claim.
Scope qualifiers — slot 1 polish
indépendamment de
regardless of
Strongest universaliser. + noun. e.g. indépendamment du niveau de revenus.
quel(le) que soit
whatever / regardless of what
Agrees with noun: quel que soit l'âge / quelle que soit la situation.
à condition de / à condition que
on the condition that / provided that
à condition de + infinitive. à condition que + subjunctive.
sans pour autant
without necessarily going so far as to
+ infinitive. Limits the main claim. High register.
pour peu que + subjonctif
provided that / as long as
Signals the positive outcome depends on a condition.
Example signals — slot 3 openers (rotate all three)
c'est le cas de
this is the case for / take the example of
Default slot 3 opener.
il suffit de penser à
one only needs to think of
Slightly more rhetorical than c'est le cas de.
par exemple
for example
Simplest. Never use twice in same paragraph.
Causation
ce qui
which (consequence clause)
Links state to consequence. ce qui pourrait… / ce qui entraîne…
entraîner
to lead to / to bring about
More formal than provoquer. Subject causes the outcome.
Comparison & contrast
contrairement à
contrary to / unlike
Introduces a contrast. Often sets up a nuance or counter-argument.
par rapport à
compared to / in relation to
Neutral comparison. Can be positive or negative.
autant… autant
just as… so too / on one hand… on the other
Parallel structure for two-sided argument. High register.
non seulement… mais aussi
not only… but also
Escalates an argument by adding a second layer.
Restriction nuance — use in slot 4 or nuance slot
ne signifie pas que
does not mean that
Limits the scope of a previous claim.
ne garantit pas
does not guarantee
Honest limitation — outcome is uncertain.
ne suffit pas de
it is not enough to
Signals the proposed solution is incomplete alone.
Amplifying nuance
va bien au-delà de
goes well beyond
Amplifies — shows larger scope than expected.
ne se limite pas à
is not limited to
Opens up a broader consequence than the obvious one.
non seulement possible mais aussi rentable
not only possible but also profitable
Template for showing a solution is both feasible and beneficial.
